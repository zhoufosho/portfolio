Hi I'm Elaine!

Born and raised in Orlando, Florida, I've become a California convert. I'm currently a Junior pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science from Stanford University. My concentration is in Human-Computer Interaction, and I strive for clean, intuitive, user-centered design.

I started my career trek as a science fair geek, loving the analytical puzzles, but disliking the monotony of lab work and slow pace of progress. After coming to Stanford, I've been able to explore many newfound interests -- programming, the entrepreneurial spirit that drives the Bay, and the creativity that abounds.

I'll admit that I'm still in the process of figuring out what I want to do and how to use what I've learned. I know I have a lot of different interests, but I hope to gain more experiences in these fields, tackling tough problems in the interplay between people and technology.

Feel free to contact me at elainez@cs.stanford.edu.
